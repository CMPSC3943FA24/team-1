Help Center

How do I create an account?

To create an account, go to the Sign-Up page and fill out the registration form with your email, password, and other required details. Once submitted, you'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

What if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

How do I report a power outage?

To report a power outage, navigate to the report section, select the outage type, provide details, and submit the report. Our team will review and take action as soon as possible.

What should I do if the app isn’t loading properly?

If the app isn’t loading, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, contact our support team for further assistance.
