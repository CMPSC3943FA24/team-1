Preventative Maintenance Request Information

Preventative maintenance (PM) is different from an outage. PM is scheduled work to maintain or upgrade equipment and may require temporarily shutting off power to ensure safe conditions.

An outage is an unplanned event caused by equipment failure, storms, or other factors, resulting in the interruption of power.

Preventative Maintenance: Typically scheduled in advance and communicated to customers. Helps reduce the likelihood of future outages.

Outages: Can occur suddenly, and our teams respond as quickly as possible to restore power.
